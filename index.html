Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with us.

Is this just "AI tools"?

No. We map the workflow, connect your tools, build the automation, test it, and hand over a working system.

What do you actually deliver?

AI automations, chatbots, AI agents, dashboards, UI/UX design, integrations, and deployment support.

What happens on the first call?

We identify your highest-friction workflows and recommend what to automate or redesign first.

How do we start?

Start with discovery and a process audit. From there, Team OMGuys creates a practical build plan.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope, integrations, support needs, and whether you need automation, design, build, or all three.

How do you work with our current tools?

We integrate with tools such as WordPress, Shopify, CRMs, n8n, Make, Zapier, and custom APIs.

How fast will we see impact?

Focused automations can create impact quickly; larger systems are built in milestones.

Do we need to be technical?

No. We handle mapping, design, build, testing, deployment, and handover.
